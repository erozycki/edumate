Try reading the overview to get a basic understanding of what Edumate does, or jump right in by creating an account!

How to use Edumate

Once you have an account, subscribe to one or more decks on the browse decks page. Decks are composed of flashcards, which are scheduled for review by Edumate. You can view your decks, see what is due, and initiate review sessions on the my decks page.

Each card consists of a prompt and an answer. The prompt is presented first - try to answer it as best as you can, and then press any key to reveal the answer. Afterward, indicate your performance: press "Wrong" if you got the item wrong, "Right" if you got it right, and "Trivial" if the item was so easy that you don't think you'll need to review it very often.
